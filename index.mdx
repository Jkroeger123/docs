## What is HiveSight?

HiveSight is a social listening and creator discovery platform that helps businesses:

- **Discover relevant creators** by analyzing conversations across Reddit and LinkedIn
- **Build ecosystems** of influencers and thought leaders in your industry
- **Track engagement signals** to identify the best opportunities to connect
- **Monitor communities** to stay on top of trending topics and discussions
