## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Create Your First Ecosystem

An ecosystem is a curated collection of creators, influencers, and thought leaders organized around a specific topic or theme.

**To create an ecosystem:**

1. Click the **"Create Ecosystem"** button in your dashboard
2. Give your ecosystem a descriptive name
3. Choose your discovery method:
   - **Topic-based**: Discover creators discussing specific topics
   - **Network-based**: Find creators in the network of a target profile
4. Configure your filters:
   - Minimum follower count
   - Minimum engagement threshold
   - Location preferences (optional)
5. Click **"Create"** to start building your ecosystem

<Tip>
  Start with broad filters and refine them as you learn more about your target
  audience.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Discover Relevant Creators

Once your ecosystem is created, HiveSight will begin discovering creators that match your criteria.

**What to expect:**

- Creator profiles with engagement metrics
- Content topics and themes they discuss
- Network connections and relationships
- Recent posts and activity

Browse through discovered creators and add the most relevant ones to your ecosystem.

### Step 3: Set Up Tracking & Alerts

Stay informed about important engagement signals with tracking and alerts.

**Available tracking options:**

- **Directory Signals**: Get notified when target profiles or companies engage with content
- **Community Monitoring**: Track specific subreddits for relevant discussions
- **Slack Integration**: Receive real-time alerts in your Slack workspace
- **Email Digests**: Get periodic summaries of ecosystem activity

<Warning>
  Some features may require a paid subscription. Check your plan details for
  available features.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Engage with Your Audience

Use the insights from HiveSight to:

- Identify high-value conversations to join
- Find the right creators to partner with
- Understand trending topics in your industry
- Track competitor mentions and discussions
